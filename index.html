Hi there! I'm Paul.

I am a graduate student in the School of Computing and Information Sciences (SCIS) at Florida International University (FIU), where I am pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science. I am affiliated with the Student-Athlete Academic Center (SAAC) at FIU, where I am currently a tutor for student athletes. I am a Google CS First instructor (formerly igniteCS), teaching the next generation the basics of computer science and engineering. I am also serving as a mentor via the UPE Mentor program at FIU (formerly known as MentorFIU), where I provide guidance, support, and critical resources to a younger student so that they can succeed from the start of their collegiate journey.

Most recently, I participating in the Gydable Winter of Code. I am working with two other students and one mentor on a mental health web application that we will be demoing in February.

Interests: I am interested in software engineering, data science, web development, GPS/GIS, and CS education.
