Hi there! I'm Paul.

I am an undergraduate student in the School of Computing and Information Sciences (SCIS) at Florida International University (FIU), where I study computer science. I am affiliated with the Student-Athlete Academic Center (SAAC) at FIU, where I am currently a tutor for student athletes. I am a Google CS First instructor (formerly igniteCS), teaching the next generation the basics of computer science and engineering. I am also serving as a mentor this semester via the UPE Mentor program at FIU (formerly known as MentorFIU), where I provide guidance, support, and critical resources to a younger student so that they can succeed from the start of their collegiate journey. I previously worked at Hard Rock Stadium as the press elevator attendant, where I took some of the most well-known voices and writers in pro sports to their respective booths.

Interests: I am interested in data science, web development, GPS/GIS, and CS education.
